About

Originally from the small town of Smiths Falls, Jared DeVries is a Toronto-based filmmaker working as a Camera Operator, Writer, and Director.

His work explores loneliness, religion, dark humour, and Canadian culture through narrative filmmaking. Jared has worked on independent films and student productions while developing his own writing and directing projects.
